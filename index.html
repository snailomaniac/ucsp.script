 Sector 2

Reciprocity. ang reciprocity ay parang "give and take" lamang.

dahil ang aim ng reciprocity ay patibayin ang bond ng dalawang participants.

dahil habang ang dalawang participants dumadami ang naiipong favor ng

contributors na magagamit sa mga panahong nangangailangan sila.


Utang na loob o (Dept of gratitude). is another type of reciprocity.

ito ang "kindness" o pabor na expected ibigay. wala tayong batas ukol dito

pero inaasan nating na kapag nagbigay ng tulog ang isang tao magaantay

tayo ng may ibibigay na kapalit, kaya nagkakaroon tayo ng walang utang

na loob kapag hindi mo to ginawa.

 Sector 3

- First Generalized Reciprocity

Is a exchange of good and services without a definite time frame of

when the favor should be retured.

(Next Slide)

- Then Balanced Reciprocity

The exchange occurs between groups or individuals with the donor

expecting to recieve something equal or similar value.

(Next Slide)

- And Negative Reciprocity

This happens when group try to maximize their gains while giving as

little as possible.
